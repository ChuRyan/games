Hector the Hippo

Hector the Hippo is an educational game built during the Cupertino Library's 2016 Summer Hackathon, winning the first prize. The goal of the game is to sort waste into the right bins; after two or three playthroughs, players will be able to have a great understanding about where to place their trash. Four different categories are used in this game: Trash, Recyclables, Yard Waste/Compost, and Special Cases. This game was built using a coding language similar to JavaScript. The graphics and animations were created in Blender 3D.

RWBY Tower Brawlers

RWBY Tower Brawlers is a real-time tower fighting game, taking in inspiration from the new genre of MOBA games, as well as the fighting style of the Super Smash Bros. series. Players try to knock their opponents out of the stage and then advance towards their objective, while preventing their opponents from advancing. The game supports online multiplayer and LAN connections. Created during the summer of 2016.

The game is a fangame based off of the webseries RWBY owned by Rooster Teeth. I have no affiliation with Rooster Teeth; the game was made for educational and entertainment purposes.
